About Bot Studio for Discord (BSD)

Bot Studio for Discord is a program for creating private or public Discord Bots. It's the easiest way to create Discord Bots. A drag-and-drop editor makes it simple to create the perfect bot for your servers - all without programming knowledge!

How to Download Mods

Follow these steps to download your wanted mods:

  1. Go to the Mods page.
  2. Search the Mod you want to download.
  3. Click on the Mod you want, and press the "download" button.

How to Upload Mods

Follow these steps to upload your mods:

  1. Add "Aqusorias" on Discord.
  2. Provide following information: Name, File & Description of the Mod.
  3. Wait up to 24 hours for your Mod to go live.

How to Program a Mod

Ready to create your own mods? Here's a quick guide:

  1. Go to the Mods page.
  2. Install the "AAA_Bettermods.js" Mod.
  3. Check the "example_mod.js" Mod.
  4. Create your own .js file on your pc and go on programming!
